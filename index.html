Every new patient begins with a complimentary consultation with Dr. Rana. During this session, she assesses your skin, listens to your goals, and recommends a personalised treatment plan. There is no one-size-fits-all approach — your unique anatomy and lifestyle inform every decision.
Comfort is a priority at our clinic. Most treatments involve minimal discomfort. We apply topical numbing cream prior to injectable procedures and use the finest-gauge needles available. Our laser systems feature integrated cooling to maintain comfort throughout.
This depends on the treatment. Botox results typically appear within 3–7 days. Fillers produce immediate volume, refining over 2 weeks. Laser and PRP therapies build progressively over 4–8 weeks as natural regeneration occurs. Dr. Rana will set clear expectations at your consultation.
Most of our treatments are designed to fit seamlessly into your routine. Injectables and many laser treatments carry little to no downtime. Deeper resurfacing procedures may involve 2–5 days of redness. We will always advise you precisely before your treatment so you can plan accordingly.
We exclusively use FDA-cleared and CE-marked technologies from leading global manufacturers. Our injectables are sourced from Allergan Aesthetics (Botox, Juvederm). Laser platforms are from Syneron-Candela and Lumenis. We never compromise on the quality of products used on our patients.
